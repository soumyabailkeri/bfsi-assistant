BANK LOAN POLICY DOCUMENT

HOME LOAN ELIGIBILITY:
- Minimum age: 21 years, Maximum age: 65 years at loan maturity
- Minimum income: Rs 25,000 per month for salaried employees
- Minimum income: Rs 2,00,000 per year for self-employed
- Maximum loan amount: 80% of property value
- Minimum employment: 2 years in current job
- Credit score requirement: minimum 700
- Required documents: Aadhaar card, PAN card, salary slips (3 months), bank statements (6 months), property documents

PERSONAL LOAN ELIGIBILITY:
- Minimum age: 21 years, Maximum age: 60 years
- Minimum monthly income: Rs 20,000
- Minimum employment: 1 year in current organisation
- Maximum loan amount: Rs 40,00,000
- Credit score requirement: minimum 650
- Required documents: Aadhaar card, PAN card, salary slips (3 months), bank statements (3 months)

FIXED DEPOSIT POLICY:
- Minimum deposit amount: Rs 10,000
- Minimum tenure: 7 days, Maximum tenure: 10 years
- Interest rates: 5.5% to 7.5% depending on tenure
- Senior citizen additional rate: 0.50% extra
- Premature withdrawal penalty: 1% on applicable rate
- TDS applicable if interest exceeds Rs 40,000 per year

CASH WITHDRAWAL POLICY:
- Maximum cash withdrawal per day at branch: Rs 1,00,000
- Maximum ATM withdrawal per day: Rs 50,000
- Large cash transactions above Rs 50,000 require PAN card
- Cash transactions above Rs 10,00,000 reported to Income Tax department
- Teller must verify denomination count and obtain customer signature

REMITTANCE POLICY:
- Domestic NEFT: Available 24x7, minimum Rs 1, no maximum limit
- Domestic RTGS: Minimum Rs 2,00,000, available 24x7
- International remittance: Maximum USD 250,000 per year under LRS
- Required documents for international remittance: PAN card, purpose declaration
- Processing time: NEFT same day, RTGS within 30 minutes
